# **Experimental Reading**

## **Thought**

- What: How can I do experiments with reading ?
- Why: 
	- to understand how to do reading
	- to know/find/learn different ways to read different things


- What different things
	- reading for the purpose of experiments
	- reading as part of experiment
	- reading books, articles, codes
	- reading humans !!
	- reading face !!
	- reading emotions !!!
	- reading stars
	- reading hands !!
	- reading maps ?
	- reading the situation (as part of OODA loop)

- How to experiment while reading or with reading

	- experiment
		- pick a domain, goal/objective
		- make hypothesis
		- do test, make a change, take an action, tamper, perturb, then
		- do the observation, monitor,  collect data then
		- do thinking, reasoning, compare hypothesis with observation
		- conclude something, document things, then 
		- make another hypothesis
